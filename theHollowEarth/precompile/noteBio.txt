# Biography

Adam McCartney (*1987 Limerick, Ireland) studied with John Godfrey at UCC and Klaus Lang in Graz, 
Austria. He lives in Vienna where he works as a freelance composer and enjoys the cooperative
nature of this work. His music has been performed internationally, played at concerts and festivals
for contemporary music, broadcast on the radio and released on cd and vinyl.
